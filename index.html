What’s Changed in the Past Three Months

Yett has made meaningful progress across product, partnerships, and team fronts.

User Growth

We’ve crossed 25,000 downloads with 14,000 active users, with engagement time greater than 3 minutes 30 seconds.

Merchant Onboarding

Over 2,000 stores are now live across five cities — Lucknow, Jaipur, Agra, Indore, and Varanasi.

Mall Partnerships

5 malls in Jaipur, 6 in Lucknow, and 1 in Agra have been onboarded.

Enterprise Integrations

I met the Unicommerce team at their Delhi office, which provided a roadmap to bring large retail brands onto Yett through their integration layer.

Exposure and Partnerships

Attending the Global Fintech Fest in Mumbai offered valuable insights and networking opportunities with payment gateways. We are now planning an Offer and Loyalty Program in collaboration with Cootpay, a UPI-based loyalty solution.

Investor Interest

Titan Capital has shown interest. We had a detailed 45-minute discussion three weeks ago, and on follow-up last week, they mentioned they are still mulling over the opportunity.

Product Milestone

Yett 2.0 is now ready for rollout in the first week of November. An offline chatbot agent developed by Prateek’s team in Bangalore also looks very promising.

Team and Culture

Our 7-member business development and 4-member content and strategy teams are functioning cohesively. I feel far more confident now, having launched the app and seen good feedback from both users and businesses. We have also started generating small revenue from businesses since September.

Wedding Campaign

We are launching a wedding campaign (Wedding Zing) from November 15 to January 30 across jewellery, footwear, ethnic wear (men and women), accessories, and boutiques categories.

Outlook

With the upcoming features in Yett 2.0, I’m optimistic that users will find the platform even more engaging and meaningful.

Success or failure aside, people are resonating with the intent and thought behind Yett.

We are building this with commitment and dedication, and we will try not to let you down in effort, intent, or conduct.
